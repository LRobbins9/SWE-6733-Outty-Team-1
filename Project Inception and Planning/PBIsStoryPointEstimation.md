# PBIs Story Point Estimation  
The rough estimation of the current working items in the backlog is 29 story points. The story points for each task and feature were decided by considering the effort, time to develop and complexity. In addition, special circumstances were considered such as the use of IDE AI tools to aid in the completion of the tasks and features.

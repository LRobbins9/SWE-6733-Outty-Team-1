# Product Vision      

## Near Vision   
The near vision of Outty focuses the user experience and stable critical features. These features will aide the users in finding suitables matches that have similar outdoor adventures to them, leading to new connections and possible lasting relationships. These critical features include the following: 
- Enabling the user to manage their account (creation/deletion) with a username and password combination.
- Allowing the user to upload photos to their profile.
- A matching algorithm that takes in the users outdoor adventure preferences and uses that to determine a suitable match.
- The ability for the user to link their social media accounts.
- In-app messaging between connected users. 

## Far Vision  
The far vision of Outty aims to further enhance the user experience and add additional security and safety features. These types of features would be developed and added as patches and features updates after an initial release. The enhanced features include the following: 
- Integrating maps that suggest activities such as various hiking trails, lakes, etc.
- A collaboration section that would allow third party individuals to offer deals on outdoor adventures such as kayaking for two or a rock climbing class.
- Background checks on matched connections. 

## Stakeholders  

<b> 1. End Users </b>  
End users include individuals wanting to use the app to find a connection.  

<b> 2. Product Owner and Development Team </b>  
This includes anyone who has a hand in planning, managing and developing the Outty app.  

<b> 3. Third Party </b>  
This stakeholders would be considered in the far vision of the Outty app. They include small business owners and private security companies.
